Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Cook What You Have. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

1. Information We Collect

a) User Profile Information

  • We do not collect email addresses or passwords.
  • Users may set a username and choose an avatar — stored only on your device.
  • Profile data never leaves your device.

b) User Input Data

  • Ingredients you enter in the app
  • Recipes you save or mark as favorites
  • Ratings you give to recipes
  • All of this data is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage) only.

c) Technical Information

  • No personal data, usage logs, or analytics are collected by us.
  • We do not track how you use the app.

2. Camera & Device Permissions

This app does not use your camera or request any camera permission.

  • No camera access is requested or used at any point.
  • No photos are captured, stored, or transmitted.
  • The image scan feature has been removed from this version.

3. Local Data Storage

All your data — saved recipes, favorites, your name, avatar, and ratings — is stored only on your device using localStorage.

  • This data is never sent to any server.
  • You can clear it anytime via Settings → Clear History.
  • Uninstalling or clearing browser data removes everything.

4. AI Recipe Generation

When you tap "Find Recipes with AI", your ingredient list is sent to an AI service to generate recipe suggestions.

  • Only ingredient names are shared — no personal information.
  • No data is stored by us after the response is received.
  • This feature requires an internet connection.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • AI services (for recipe generation and Hindi translation of steps)
  • Unsplash (for food images — no personal data shared)
  • Google Fonts (for typography — standard CDN request)

We do not use advertising networks or sell any data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party. Ingredient data sent for AI generation is transient and not stored by us.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights & Control

  • You can delete all your data anytime using Settings → Clear History.
  • Stop using the app anytime — no account deletion needed.
  • Your data belongs to you and stays on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

📧 Email: sarimforbusiness@gmail.com

11. Consent

By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
